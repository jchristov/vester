Algorithmic Trading for the rest of us.

Vester is an easy-to-use framework for developing and running your own algorithmic trading systems. It is built on Redux and inspired by LMAX.

One-Way Data Flow

Algorithms are straight forward, easy to understand, and just as easy to debug.

Slack Notifications

Don’t let your gains come unnoticed. Simply add your Slack API endpoint and Vester will notify you whenever a new event is dispatched.

Multiple Order Types

Be it limit orders, market orders or percentage orders, Vester makes order execution a breeze.

Built for Developers

Not everyone is a post-doc in quantitative finance, and with Vester no one has to be.

Backtesting Metrics

Behind any successful algorithm lies thorough backtesting. With Vester’s built-in metrics, backtesting becomes as fun as it should be.

Flourishing Community

Get the most out of your algorithmic trading by sharing your tips and ideas with others.
